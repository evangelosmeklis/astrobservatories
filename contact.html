Hi. I am Evangelos Meklis, a Software Engineer based in Athens, Greece, and an astronomy & astrophotography enthusiast — the creator and maintainer of Astrobservatories.

Is an observatory missing from the map? Did you spot wrong details — coordinates, telescopes, history, Bortle class? I'd love to hear about it.

Send an email to vaggelis98.work@gmail.com with the observatory's name and, if you can, a link or source for the correct information. Every verified report makes the map better.

To help me sort reports quickly, please start your subject line with what the email is about, for example:

  • “Addition of [observatory name]” — for observatories missing from the map
  • “Details correction — [observatory name]” — for wrong or outdated information
  • “Improvement idea — [your idea]” — for suggestions to make the website better

There is no contact form — plain email works best. Clicking an example above opens your email app with the subject pre-filled.
